Randomization

Have picked 10215 ORF randomly from hg38 genome (GENCODE V35 Comprehensive) .Then we look for the overlapping alu from 10215 randomly picked ORF .T-Test was done on the randomized data and the original data .This process was repeated 10000 times .The minimum pvalue = 4.38 * 10^(-31) .The maximum pvalue = 5.12 * 10^(-15)
